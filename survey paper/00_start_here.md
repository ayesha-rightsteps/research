# 👋 Hey Ayesha — Read This First

## What is this paper about?
This paper is a comprehensive survey of **Deep Reinforcement Learning (DRL)** — a powerful AI technique — and how it is being used to make machines smarter and more autonomous. The researchers reviewed dozens of recent studies to map out how DRL is being applied across four major autonomous systems: self-driving cars, robots, drones, and Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS). They highlight what's working, what challenges remain, and where the field is headed next.

## Why does it matter?
Autonomous systems — cars that drive themselves, robots that work in factories, drones that deliver packages — are rapidly entering our everyday world, and DRL is the engine powering many of their most critical decisions. This survey is important because it gives researchers, engineers, and policymakers a single, organized resource to understand the state of the art, identify gaps in current technology, and focus future efforts on solving real-world safety and deployment challenges.
